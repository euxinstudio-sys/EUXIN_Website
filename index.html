AXRED

AXRED is a stealth-action video game set in our current times.

The fight for the soul betwen the young and the old. You are your worst enemy. Can you defeat yourself?

Another name for the world is the AXIS. This synonim represents the 3-dimensional(XYZ) realm of form and direction, the space through which passions travel.

The AXIS also takes the shape of an military organisation established by the demons as a means of killing the souls of people before they physically die.

Vali, a teenager, in his pursuit of overcoming the world, is being offered by the Old-Man, in other words his old-self, that if he bows to the powers of evil, he will be given the chance of becoming the ANTI, a distorted, false figure of God, and put an end to the world.

The soul loves the body, yet the body hates the soul. Vali finds himself in a state of constant and agonizing despair. Crushed by temptations from the Old-Man, he starts seeing the world differently.

The story touches the themes of self-reflection, depression, anxiety, addiction, violence and the broken home.

The main objective is not settling for second best, but for the Ultimate. The life of the world to come.

Do not let the old become the young.

Note: The game is still in development.
